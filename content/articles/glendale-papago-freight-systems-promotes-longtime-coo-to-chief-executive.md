The announcement is the latest sign of momentum for Glendale's business community, which has drawn growing attention from investors across the Southwest.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Ashley White, chief operating officer in Glendale.

The company now employs roughly 39 people across its Arizona operations, with plans to keep hiring if growth holds through next year. Leadership says Glendale's talent pipeline was a deciding factor in choosing to expand locally rather than out of state.

> "We're proud of what this means for Glendale and the people who live here." — Ashley White

Company leadership says an update on hiring and expansion plans is expected before the end of the year.
