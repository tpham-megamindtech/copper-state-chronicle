The news comes as wellness businesses across Glendale report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"It's a big step forward for Glendale, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Ashley White, clinic founder.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 24 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 11 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Ashley White

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
