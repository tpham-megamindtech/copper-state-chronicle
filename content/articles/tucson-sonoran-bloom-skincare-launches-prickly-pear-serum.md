The news comes as wellness businesses across Tucson report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Nicole Davis, licensed esthetician in Tucson.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 16 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 3 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Tucson and the people who live here." — Nicole Davis

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
