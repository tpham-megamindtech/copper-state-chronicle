"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Kevin Johnson, board chair in Kingman.

The company now employs roughly 48 people across its Arizona operations, with plans to keep hiring if growth holds through next year. Leadership says Kingman's talent pipeline was a deciding factor in choosing to expand locally rather than out of state.

> "We're proud of what this means for Kingman and the people who live here." — Kevin Johnson

Company leadership says an update on hiring and expansion plans is expected before the end of the year.
