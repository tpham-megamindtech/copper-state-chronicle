The news comes as wellness businesses across Scottsdale report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"We've put a lot of work into getting this right, and people are already noticing the difference," said Laura Ramirez, clinic founder in Scottsdale.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 14 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 13 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "This is exactly the kind of progress our community deserves." — Laura Ramirez

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
