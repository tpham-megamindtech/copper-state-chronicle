The news comes as wellness businesses across Prescott report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"We've put a lot of work into getting this right, and people are already noticing the difference," said Robert Jackson, master stylist in Prescott.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 23 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 10 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "This is exactly the kind of progress our community deserves." — Robert Jackson

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
