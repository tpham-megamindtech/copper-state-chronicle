The news comes as wellness businesses across Kingman report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"It's a big step forward for Kingman, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said Emily Clark, spa owner.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 30 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 5 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — Emily Clark

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
