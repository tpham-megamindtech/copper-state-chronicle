The news comes as wellness businesses across Casa Grande report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"It's a big step forward for Casa Grande, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said David Delgado, wellness director.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 27 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 14 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — David Delgado

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
