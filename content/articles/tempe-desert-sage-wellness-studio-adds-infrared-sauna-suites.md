The news comes as wellness businesses across Tempe report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Tyler Moore, clinic founder in Tempe.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 19 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 6 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Tempe and the people who live here." — Tyler Moore

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
