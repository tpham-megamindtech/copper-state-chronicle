The news comes as wellness businesses across Gilbert report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"It's a big step forward for Gilbert, and it wouldn't have happened without a lot of people pulling in the same direction," said James Anderson, licensed esthetician.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 21 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 8 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "It took teamwork, but we got there." — James Anderson

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
