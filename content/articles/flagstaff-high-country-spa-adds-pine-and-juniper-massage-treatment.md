The news comes as wellness businesses across Flagstaff report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Maria Reyes, wellness director in Flagstaff.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 22 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 9 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Flagstaff and the people who live here." — Maria Reyes

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
