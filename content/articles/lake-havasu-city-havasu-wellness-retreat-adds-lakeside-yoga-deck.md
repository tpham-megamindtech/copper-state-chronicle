The news comes as wellness businesses across Lake Havasu City report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"This is something our community has been asking for, and we're glad to finally see it come together," said Amanda Garcia, master stylist in Lake Havasu City.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 28 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 3 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "We're proud of what this means for Lake Havasu City and the people who live here." — Amanda Garcia

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
