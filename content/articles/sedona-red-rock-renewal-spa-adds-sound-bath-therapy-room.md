The news comes as wellness businesses across Sedona report steady demand from both longtime regulars and a growing number of visitors drawn to the desert lifestyle.

"We've put a lot of work into getting this right, and people are already noticing the difference," said Andrew Torres, wellness director in Sedona.

Staff say appointment requests have climbed roughly 17 percent since word spread locally. The business currently employs 4 licensed practitioners and plans to add more positions if demand keeps pace.

> "This is exactly the kind of progress our community deserves." — Andrew Torres

Those interested can book online or by phone, and walk-ins are welcome as availability allows.
